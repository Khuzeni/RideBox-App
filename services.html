Our Deployment Strategy

The RideBox Pilot Program

A 21-unit national pilot targeting high-traffic nodes across South Africa. Three strategic locations per province: regional airports, university campuses, and shopping malls.

Three Strategic Nodes

Deployment Locations

01
Regional Airports
Units to be deployed at the largest airport in each province. Targeting international tourists and business travelers who face friction with foreign currency and unfamiliar pricing. AI-assisted price comparison and automatic currency conversion protect visitors from price gouging.
02
University Campuses
Units to be at the largest university in each province. Serving students who regularly experience dead batteries and depleted data after long days of classes or social events. Immediate access to transportation without requiring a charged personal device.
03
Shopping Malls
Units to be deployed at the largest shopping mall in each province. Capturing high foot-traffic from shoppers who need reliable transit home. Solves the problem of hands full, phone dead, or unsure about calling a ride in unfamiliar areas.
04
Vetted Driver Network
Every driver will undergo facial verification, background checks, and continuous review. Transparent earnings per trip. Direct support access for drivers on the road. Accountability from both sides of every journey.
05
Safety Architecture
Facial verification at booking time. SOS panic button on every unit. Real-time trip tracking. AI sentiment analysis to flag problematic profiles before dispatch. Emergency response partners on standby 24/7.
06
Digital Inclusion
All 11 official South African languages supported via AI interface. Fixed fares with no surge pricing. Payment through multiple channels including Yoco integration. No smartphone required to book a ride.
